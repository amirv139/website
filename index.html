IntraAI: Simplifying the Internship Hiring Process

AI-powered platform for universities, colleges, and companies to find the best interns efficiently.

About IntraAI

IntraAI is an innovative platform designed to streamline the internship application and hiring process. Our web application connects universities, colleges, and companies with top-tier students and helps them efficiently evaluate and hire interns. With AI-driven assessments, you get the most qualified candidates, saving time and effort in the hiring process.

Key Benefits for Employers, Universities, and Students

For Employers

IntraAI simplifies your hiring process by giving you only the top candidates. Our AI-powered assessments evaluate students based on the job role and company description, ensuring you get the right fit without sifting through hundreds of resumes and conducting countless interviews.

For Universities

Universities can partner with IntraAI to connect their students with reputable employers. The AI-driven platform ensures that students are better prepared and matched with roles that align with their skills, ensuring successful internships for both students and employers.

For Students

Students benefit from personalized internship recommendations based on their field of study, skills, and career goals. With automated resume feedback and AI-powered assessments, students are better prepared and have a higher chance of landing internships that match their aspirations.

How IntraAI Works

  1. Employers post internship opportunities.
  2. Students upload their resumes and cover letters for analysis.
  3. Students are matched with internships that align with their skills, education, and career goals.
  4. AI-powered assessments evaluate students based on the job role.
  5. Employers receive a shortlist of top candidates based on AI assessments.
